Professional Procrastination

Professional Procrastination PP is the highly advanced skill of delaying important tasks while remaining extremely busy doing everything except the original task. It is commonly observed among students, programmers, designers, and anyone with an internet connection.

History

Historians believe procrastination began shortly after humans invented deadlines.

The first recorded procrastinator allegedly said:
I'll finish carving this wheel tomorrow.

He never did.

Characteristics

People suffering from Professionalprofessionalist Procrastination usually display one or more of the following behaviors:

  • Watching productivity videos instead of working.
  • Cleaning the room just before studying.
  • Organizing desktop folders for three hours.
  • Opening ChatGPT for homework but asking random questions first.
  • Watching How to Stop Procrastinating while procrastinating.
